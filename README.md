# ✈️ Aviation Crisis: The Impact of the US-Iran Conflict on Global Aviation (2026)

An 8-page Power BI dashboard analyzing the real-time aviation disruptions caused by the 2026 US-Iran conflict. Covers conflict strike mapping, airspace closures, airline financial losses, airport disruptions, flight reroutes, and cancellation timelines across 35 airlines and 35 airports.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Executive Summary | KPI cards — $49M in losses, 120K passengers impacted, 195 cancellations |
| Conflict Locations | Azure Maps showing strike locations by severity across the Middle East |
| Financial Losses | Airline daily losses and passengers impacted ranked by airline |
| Resilience Analysis | Bubble chart comparing airline losses vs reroutes vs cancelled flights |
| Disruptions Per Country | Stacked bar of cancellations, delays, and diversions by airport |
| Closure Duration | Airspace closure hours by country — Iran leads at 300+ hours |
| Costs & Delays | Fuel cost and delay minutes by airline from rerouted flights |
| Affected Routes | Most disrupted departure airports and routes |

---

## 🔑 Key Findings

- **$49M** in estimated daily losses across 35 airlines
- **Peak disruptions on March 1** — the day following the Feb 28 airstrikes
- **Emirates & Qatar Airways** lost the most — over $4M and $3M per day respectively
- **Iran's airspace closed for 300+ hours** — the longest closure of any country
- **120K+ passengers** directly impacted by cancellations and reroutes
- **DXB (Dubai)** was the most affected departure airport globally
